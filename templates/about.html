About



History

My name is Natalia and I come from a small Eastern European country, Moldova. My passion for creating dresses comes from childhood, when I would look for dresses in my mother's wardrobe and cut them out for a new design. My mother was very angry with everything I did because I was ruining all her dresses. After a while she started to like what I was doing and even wore them. The mother's colleagues at work admired the newly made dresses and tried to guess who the designer was. After a few years, my mother told them that the designer was my 14-year-old daughter. Everyone was amazed and no one could believe it. This is where my tailoring and design career began, making dresses for my mother's colleagues. The brand was created in 2014 and is based on handwork and high quality fabrics from Spain and Italy.

Vision

Style and Design: Our team works with clients to determine the style and design elements they want in a dress. This includes the silhouette, neckline, sleeve length, hem and any additional details such as pleats, ruffles or embellishments. Fabric Selection: We help customers choose the right fabrics for their dresses based on their desired style, occasion and personal preference. Different fabrics drape and behave differently, so your choice of fabric can greatly affect the final look and fit of the dress. Alterations: We offer alteration services to existing dresses to achieve a better fit or update the style. Common alterations include inserting or releasing seams, adjusting length, and resizing sleeves. Tailoring is important in making a well-enriched dress that enhances a woman's look and confidence. We work with clients to bring their vision to life, ensuring that the dress reflects their personal style and flatters their body shape.

Goals

Our main goal is to achieve the highest level of tailoring craftsmanship. Creating garments that are impeccably constructed, with attention to detail, precision in cut and stitching, and superior fit. We continuously improve our skills, staying up to date with the latest techniques and innovations in the field. Customer Satisfaction: Creating garments that not only meet but exceed their expectations. We work with customers to understand their requirements, needs and personal styles, providing them with comfortable finished garments that they feel confident in. Creativity and innovation: We aim to push the boundaries of design and fashion. By incorporating unique and imaginative elements into our work, experimenting with new fabrics, cuts, patterns and embellishments. In the near future we will create all the models in 3D and present them to customers to avoid the stock of unsold clothes and protect the environment.
